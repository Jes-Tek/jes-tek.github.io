Core components checklist

A practical bill of materials to get from bench to field without mystery failures.

Radio stack

  • TX/RX pair (protocol matched), antennas properly oriented
  • Telemetry channel for voltage/RSSI
  • Interference audit: Wi‑Fi, VTX, and ESC PWM noise

Motion stack

  • BLDC motors sized for thrust/weight targets
  • ESCs with current headroom and cooling
  • Gearing/prop selection aligned to mission profile

Compute & sensing

  • Onboard MCU or SBC (mission logic, mixing, logs)
  • IMU/GNSS where needed; vibration isolation
  • Failsafes: arming, loss-of-link, low-voltage landing

Power integrity

  • Separate noisy loads, add LC filters where needed
  • Calibrated voltage/current sensing
  • Harness: strain relief, heatshrink, right connectors

FPV and networking

From analog VTX to Wi‑Fi/RTSP on phones and routers — tune for bitrate, buffer, and RF coexistence.

Wi‑Fi FPV quick tips

  • Use 5 GHz for lower congestion; lock channel width to stabilize latency.
  • Pin bitrate/encoding on the sender to avoid oscillations.
  • Separate control link and video link bands to prevent desense.

Router tricks

  • Bridge modes for range; fixed IPs for low discovery time.
  • QoS to prioritize FPV stream over background chatter.
